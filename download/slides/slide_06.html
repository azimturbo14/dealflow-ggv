Before DealFlow vs After DealFlow

Current Process
  • Analyst opens each application manually
  • Spends 30-45 minutes researching basics (funding, market, team)
  • Writes notes on strengths and concerns
  • No standardized evaluation criteria — each analyst uses different judgment
  • After 2-3 weeks, the team narrows 300 applications down to 50 for interviews
  • Total time per cycle: 200+ analyst hours on screening alone
With DealFlow AI
  • Upload all 300 applications as a single CSV file
  • AI evaluates every startup in 30 seconds with consistent criteria
  • Each startup gets: confidence score, strengths, red flags, and decision logic
  • Analysts immediately see the top-ranked startups and the reasons why
  • First deep analysis meeting happens the next day, not next week
  • Total time per cycle: under 1 hour of analyst time on screening

That is a 95%+ reduction in screening time.

This is the moment the audience does the mental math. Walk through the left column slowly — let them feel the pain. Then switch to the right column and emphasize the speed. The key number is 200+ hours down to under 1 hour. That is not a small improvement — it is a fundamental shift in how the fund operates. Ask them: "What could your analysts do with 200 extra hours per cycle?"
