The Partnership — A 30-Day Pilot Program

Low risk for IT-Park, high value for both sides.

What We Need From IT-Park

  • Anonymized data on 50-100 past startup evaluations (features + outcome: success or fail). This is used to retrain the ML model on IT-Park's specific investment DNA.
  • Access for 1-2 analysts to test the tool on incoming deal flow during the pilot period.

What IT-Park Gets

  • A custom-trained DealFlow instance fine-tuned on IT-Park's historical decisions.
  • Full transparency: every score, every red flag, every decision path is visible and auditable.
  • A dedicated development team that will iterate based on analyst feedback within 48 hours.
  • At the end of 30 days: a clear report showing time saved and evaluation accuracy.

This is the ask. Be clear and specific. You are not asking for money. You are asking for data (anonymized, so there is no confidentiality concern) and a small amount of analyst time. In return, IT-Park gets a custom-built tool and a team that will iterate fast. The 30-day pilot structure reduces risk — if it does not work, they walk away. If it does, you move to a formal commercial partnership. End this slide by saying: "We are not asking you to buy software. We are asking you to test a hypothesis with us."
