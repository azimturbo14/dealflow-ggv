Conceptual UI
Let me walk you through the three steps. Step 1 — Input — is specifically designed for IT-Park's reality. Many of your accelerator applicants are early-stage with no website, no Crunchbase profile, minimal online presence. So the system doesn't require a URL. It accepts CSV uploads and manual data entry — because that's how your applications actually arrive. You only need 6 or 7 basic data points per startup. Step 2 is where the intelligence lives. An LLM extracts structured information from each application, and then a trained Decision Tree ML model evaluates it across multiple dimensions: funding history, team size, B2B versus B2C, how quickly they raised their first round, and the founder's track record. Step 3 gives your analysts everything they need in one view — a confidence score, strengths, red flags with explanations, and the full decision logic path. All exportable as CSV for your existing workflows.
