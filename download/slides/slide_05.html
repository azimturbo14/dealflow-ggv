Not a Black Box — A Transparent Decision Engine

|--- Has previous exit? --- YES --- Team size ≤ 12? --- YES → INVEST (89%)

|--- Has previous exit? --- NO --- Funding > $4.5M? --- YES --- Time to first funding ≤ 18mo? --- YES → INVEST (76%)

Decision Tree logic — fully auditable by any analyst
This is the slide that truly differentiates DealFlow from every other AI tool on the market. Most AI is a black box — you put data in, you get a score out, and nobody can tell you why. A Decision Tree is fundamentally different. You can literally read the logic, node by node, branch by branch. If IT-Park asks "why did you reject startup number 247?", the answer isn't "the AI said so." The answer is: "It was rejected because the founder has no prior exit, total funding is under $4.5 million, and it's a B2C company — and here is the raw data to prove each of those points." That level of transparency is what builds trust between your analysts and the tool. They don't have to take the system's word for it. They can verify every single decision themselves. This is AI you can audit, challenge, and understand.
